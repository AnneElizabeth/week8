Creatures from the Wheel of Time

Robert Jordan's epic 14-book fantasy series, The Wheel of Time, contains enough non-human characters to keep a fantasy zoologist busy for centuries (wouldn't that be a cool occupation?!).

The non-human characters in the series can be organized into three general categories:

  • Creatures of the Light
  • Creatures of the Dark
  • Familiar Creatures
